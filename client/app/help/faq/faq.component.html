What is the AHEM Ad Hoc Email service exactly?

AHEM is a free ad-hoc disposable temporary Email service. Ad-hoc - a mail that a mailbox is created for you as soon as you need it. Disposable - you can throw it right away. Not strings attached. Temporary - the emails recieved will be deleted by the system after a predefined time period. Free - you know what that means!
