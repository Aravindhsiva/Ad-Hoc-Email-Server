AHEM server is an ad-hoc mail server. By ad-hoc we mean that a mail box will be created for you as soon as an email for your account reaches the server. What is you account? Any account name you choose.

AHEM will check in an interval of {{properties.emailDeleteInterval | amDuration:'seconds'}} for emails that aged over {{properties.emailDeleteAge | amDuration:'seconds'}}, and if it find these, it will erase them.

So go ahead, and give it a try. Send an email to whatever@one-of-the domains above, and enter your account.
Or generate a random account.
